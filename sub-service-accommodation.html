Poojai Services

Thirukadaiyur Sivasri S.M.Kalyanam Gurukkal is a renowned priest who performs traditional rituals in the Thirukadaiyur temple. He offers services to mark important milestones in life, such as Ayush Homam, Mrityunjaya Shanti, Ugraratha Shanthi, Shastiaptha Poorthi, Bheema Ratha Shanthi, Vijayaratha Shanthi, Sadhabishegam, Kanagabhiahekam, and Mrithyunjaya Upasana Japam. These rituals have been passed down through generations of Gurukkals and are believed to bring blessings of good health, longevity, and prosperity. You can experience the power of these time-honored traditions and receive their benefits by choosing Thirukadaiyur Sivasri S.M.Kalyanam Gurukkal for your important life events.

Kalyanam Gurukkal

S.M.Kalyanam Gurukkal has been serving as an Archakar at the Shri Abirami Amman sametha Amirthakadesa Swami temple in Thirukkadaiyur for the past 50 years. He is the 7th generation of his family to perform shanti homams and archakar duties in the temple. With his expertise in Veda Sivagama, he performs rituals in the prescribed format, calling upon other scholars to assist him. He has conducted important rituals for prominent For Enquiries ities in Thirukkadaiyur and abroad. S.M.Kalyanam Gurukkal is a devout worshipper and a firm believer in preserving ancient traditions.
